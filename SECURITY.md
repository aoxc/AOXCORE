## 🛰️ Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a potential vulnerability within the AOXCORE Sovereign Fleet, we appreciate your efforts to disclose it to us in a responsible manner. 

### 🛡️ Submission Process
Do not open a Public Issue for security vulnerabilities. Instead, please follow our **Zero-Exposure** reporting protocol:

1. **Direct Communication:** Send a detailed report to `security@aoxcore.com` or via our encrypted [Sentinel Dispatch].
2. **Technical Details:** Include a summary of the vulnerability, a Proof of Concept (PoC), and the potential impact on the Sovereign Fleet.
3. **Response Timeline:** Our security team will acknowledge your report within **24 hours** and provide a full technical assessment within **72 hours**.

### 💎 Security Incentives
AOXCORE values the contribution of independent security researchers. Valid, high-impact vulnerability reports may be eligible for:
* **Hall of Fame:** Inclusion in the AOXCORE Neural Audit credits.
* **X-Layer Rewards:** Potential bounties for critical kernel-level exploits.
